About

In 2011 EDF won a the UK's largest supply contract for the Government Procurement Service providing energy related additional goods, works and services to the NHS, MoD, Metropolitan Police, London Underground and other bodies across central and local government. This site was to work as an information hub for all customer enquiries reducing the number of enquries by phone, as well as to inform customers of the benefits of using the GPS (now CCS) framework.
