About

Assembly Rooms Edinburgh were looking for a site update that improved user journey, provided a more contemporary feel, and made it obvious what their business provided to the customer. The existing site's confused elements, and distracting moving parts were improved in the new design by implementing the use of a new grid, colour coordination, and more structured information.
