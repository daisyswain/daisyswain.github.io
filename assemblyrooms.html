Assembly Rooms

About

The Assembly Rooms Edinburgh were looking for a site refresh that had a more contemporary style and an improved user journey that made it obvious what they provide to the customer. The existing site suffered from a an array of confused elements with too many moving parts so the new design concentrated on implementing the use of a grid, more clearly defined areas of colour, and more concise blocks of information.
